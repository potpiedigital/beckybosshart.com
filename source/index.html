About

Becky Bosshart is a 2015-2016 Fulbright grantee to Romania and a graduate of the Master's International Creative Writing Program at the University of Nevada, Las Vegas after serving with the Peace Corps in Ukraine from 2012 to 2014. She is the 2016 So To Speak Fiction Contest winner, judged by Emily St. John Mandel, and a 2015 Virginia Quarterly ReviewWriters' Conference fiction scholar. Her stories have appeared in Hobart and The Literary Review online, Psychopomp, and NANO Fiction. She is a reader for Creative Nonfictionmagazine.
